My journey with interior design industry started 25 years ago when together with my wife we decided to open a shop in Elmegade. We began with selling vintage furniture from the 20’s up to 80’s. I’m train a blacksmith, so fixing up second hand stuff brought me a lot of joy and satisfaction. At some point I decided not only to fix things, but also start creating brand new items. I began to improve the concepts that had potential, but were executed in a very poorly way. Then tried to change things, twist them, and in the end turn into perfection. At first, I was designing pieces of furniture to place them in my own house. So my aim was to create something that both him and his wife are going to be happy for and would be pleasing to look at in their own household. After making my first pieces, I started displaying them in his shop’s window. Since Elmegade is located in a very central spot of the city, I got an instant response on my designs. People started stopping by, asking about the products and that’s how I began to make my first sales.

Currently I’m looking for more producers interested in collaborating with me and for creating a full interior collection.
