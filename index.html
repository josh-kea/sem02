My Story

My journey with interior design industry started 25 years ago when together with my wife we decided to open a shop in Elmegade. We began with selling vintage furniture from the 20’s up to 80’s. I’m a trained blacksmith, so fixing up second hand stuff brought me a lot of joy and satisfaction. Now I create new brand new products.

Currently I’m looking for more producers interested in collaborating with me and for creating a full interior collection.

My journey with interior design industry started 25 years ago when together with my wife we decided to open a shop in Elmegade. We began selling vintage furniture from the 20’s up to 80’s. I'm a trained' blacksmith, so fixing up second hand stuff brought me a lot of joy and satisfaction. At some point I decided not only to fix things, but to also start creating brand new items. I began to improve the concepts that had potential, but were executed in a very poor way. I tried to change things, twist them, and in the end turn them into perfection. At first, I was designing pieces of furniture to place them in my own house. After making my first few pieces, I started displaying them in my shop window. Since Elmegade is located in a very central spot of the city, I got an instant response on my designs. People started stopping by, asking about the products and that’s how I began to make my first collaborations.

Currently I’m looking for more producers interested to collaborate with me and create a full interior collection.
